Don't know where to start? Try this:

With AttackSrfc you don't have to setup a scanner to get your vulnerability and patch management processes going.

Our scanless vulnerability management workflow allows you to get started immediately. You don't have to setup a vulnerability scanner to get going.

Scanners will find vulnerabilities on your systems, but not give you a full mitigation workflow. CSTOOL.io does not rely on a vulnerability scanner, but picks up where a scanner usually stops. In fact, you can use the results from your vulnerability scanner in CSTOOL.io to improve your results!

Furthermore, a security scan of a system will give you a detailed view of the vulnrabilities present on that system - but what about systems you cannot scan, such as vendors, partners or SaaS solutions? You can use CSOOLS.io to include your 3rd parties in your mitigation workflow. You can even import results from vulnerability or inventory scans! Use CSTOOLS.io mitigation workflow to assign vulnerabilities to responsible personnel and get proof of mitigation.
